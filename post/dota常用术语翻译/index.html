Dota常用术语翻译

游戏翻译

  • 面伤”aoe” - area if effect; the radius of a certain area.
  • 魔瓶”bottle” - to seal a rune into the item, Bottle.
  • 素质 “bg” - bad game; game was not fun. If a player states bg in the middle of a game, they are either being sarcastic or gave up on the hopes of winning.
  • 后期”carry” - A hero that has the capability of wiping out the enemy team and winning the game. Its also seen as “to carry”, where someone will purposely pick a “carry (hero that can carry)”, so that their chances of winning are higher.
  • 团战”clash” - a fight between both sides of the team involving all players.
  • 小兵，野怪”creeps” - the waves of units in every lane, also includes the ones in jungle camps. Also called “minions” by those who played League of Legends.
  • 反补”deny” - to have the killing blow on a friendly creep, tower, or hero so you can prevent enemy heroes from gaining the full gold and experience rewards.
  • 控制”disabler” - a hero role whose purpose is to stun or slow down an enemy hero or team.
  • 输出”dps” - damage per second; It is also used to describe heroes whose role is to deal large amounts of damage.
  • 前期”early game” - describes the part of the game that is at the very start. It is commonly called the laning phase, where everyone just sits in the lanes or jungle and obtain gold.
  • 刷”farm” - to obtain gold for the sole purpose of buying strong items. Achieved by killing creeps (minions), towers (turrets), or enemy heroes (champions). (e.g “just farm up.”)
  • 送”feed” - to constantly die and give the enemy team gold. It can either be on accident, or on purpose. “Feeding” can also occur if you do nothing in your lane and let the enemy hero in that lane farm freely.
  • 战争迷雾 “fog” - the areas you can’t see on your minimap or camera’s field of vision.
  • 泉水”fountain” - the area near the item shop that heals you. It is also the actual building that heals you and attacks enemy units that enter within its attack range.
  • 抓人”gank” - to sneak up on an enemy champion to kill them, most situations of a gank means the hero that is being killed is outnumbered.
  • 骚扰”harrass” - to auto attack or use spells against an enemy hero for the sake of making them lose hit points. it is also seen as “poke”.
  • 抱团”initiate” - to start a clash.
  • 绕树林”juke” - to trick someone into going another direction while you escape alive (e.g “you just got juked!”)
  • 抢人头”ks” - kill steal; to have a killing blow on an enemy hero that you didn’t deserve. it is often also seen as “kill secured”, a defense to someone accusing you of ksing them.
  • 正补”last hit” - to hit an enemy creep (minion) or tower (turret) and succesfully making it so that your attack was the killing blow.
  • 大后期”late game” - describes the part of the game that is close to ending, generally everyone has full builds, the best items they can possibly get, and where carrys shine.
  • miss “mia” - missing in action; used to say that someone from their lane is currently not there and may either be trying to kill a hero from a different lane, at the fountain healing and or buying items, or jungling. It is also seen as “ss”.
  • 瞬秒”nuke” - to obliterate a target quickly. Some heroes have a role called “nuker”, where their job is to nuke.
  • 没蓝”oom” - out of mana; no mana to cast any spells.
  • 被动技能”passive” - a heroes spell that is not clickable. Passives are either an aura, a chance on hit effect, or activated by an external value. However, passive can also mean to not be aggressive in a lane (being passive means to just focus on farming).
  • 推进”push” - to force your waves of creep to overwhelm the enemy team’s waves of units so that your waves will be deeper in the enemy territory. Some heroes have a role called “pusher”, where their main purpose is to push out multiple enemy lanes.
  • 推掉兵营”rax” - to destroy the enemy barracks (e.g “rax it”)
  • 回 “re” - returned; the enemy hero on their lane is no longer missing in action and can be seen in the lane.
  • 肉”tank” - a hero role whose purpose is to take all the damage for the team
  • 瞎 “tunnel vision” - to focus on one thing and not pay attention to your surroundings.
  • 野店”secret shop” - the item shop not located near the fountain (it isn’t really secret). This does not include the two shops on both top lane and bot lane.
  • 指向性技能”skill shot” - a spell that involves a player to aim rather than to just click a target or to have it instantly done for you.
  • 辅助”support” - a hero role that is not meant to kill, but to help your team get kills. generally the hero with the most utility spells.
  • 大招”ult” - ultimate; a heroes spell first learned at level 6. It can also be seen as “ss”, meaning super special.
